X Likes Gallery — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 03, 2026 · Version 1.1

Overview

X Likes Gallery is a browser extension that helps you collect, view, and organize images and videos from posts you have liked on X (Twitter). Core collection and gallery features run entirely on your device. Optional Pro features (cloud backup and subscription) transmit limited data to third-party services you choose to use, as described below. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Data We Access

To build your gallery, the Extension reads post metadata and media (images and video thumbnails/URLs) from the X pages you visit and from X's internal APIs. This includes the author handle, post text, timestamps, and media URLs of posts you have liked. This data is used solely to display and organize your collection.

How Data Is Stored

Your collected gallery data is stored locally on your device in the browser's extension storage (chrome.storage.local). It is not transmitted to us. Two optional Pro features involve external storage that you explicitly enable:

  • Cloud backup: your selected media is uploaded to your own Google Drive. We do not host your media on our servers.
  • Subscription verification: the email address you provide at checkout is stored by our payment and verification providers (see below).

Permissions Explained

  • storage / unlimitedStorage — save your gallery data locally.
  • tabs — coordinate the collector and open the gallery dashboard.
  • identity — sign in to your Google account for the optional cloud backup feature.
  • Host access to X (x.com, twitter.com) and X media/CDN hosts — read liked posts and fetch their media.
  • Host access to Google APIs — upload backups to your Google Drive (only when cloud backup is enabled).

Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with X's internal APIs to fetch post metadata and media, subject to X's Privacy Policy.

Google Drive (optional, Pro): If you enable cloud backup, your selected media is uploaded to your own Google Drive using the restricted drive.file scope, which limits access to files this app creates. We never host your media on our servers; files live only in your Drive. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Paddle (payment): Pro subscriptions are processed by Paddle.com Market Ltd. as Merchant of Record. Payment details are entered on Paddle's hosted checkout and handled by Paddle; we never receive or store your card data. Subject to Paddle's Privacy Policy.

Cloudflare Workers (subscription verification): To confirm an active subscription, the email address you provide at checkout is stored in a Cloudflare Workers KV store and queried by the extension. It is used solely for subscription verification.

Payment & Subscription Data

When you subscribe to Pro, you provide an email address used to identify your subscription. This email is stored by our payment provider (Paddle) and our verification service (Cloudflare Workers) and is used only to confirm your subscription status. You can request deletion of this data at nemolab.official@gmail.com.

Data Retention & Deletion

Gallery data remains on your device until you delete it or remove the Extension. Cloud backup files remain in your Google Drive until you delete them there. When you cancel your subscription or send a deletion request to nemolab.official@gmail.com, we remove the subscription email associated with your account from our verification store.

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction) and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the Extension evolves. Material changes will be reflected by a new effective date and version above.
